THE PAPER MIXTAPE
SCREAMS CULTURE

The Paper Mixtape is about locality. Since its premier in 2015, UCLA students have set themselves to the task of exploring Los Angeles culture and their position within it. This magazine—as reporters, fictionists, photographers, designers, artists—does not deny its predispositions or biases, but seeks to test them and grow.

Our environment is not made for consumption. We hope that you can learn with us as we make contact with our place.
